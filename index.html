TOP SPORTS AROUND THE WORLD

Different Sports are famous in different regions of the world but a few of them have received great popularity all over the world.The three top sports in the world right now are :
  1. Football
  2. Cricket
  3. Formula One

FOOTBALL

Football is played in almost all the countries of the world.However it is most popular in the continent of EUROPE.Football is played all over the european continent from countries like england and france to Monaco.Some some American countries like Argentina and Brazil are also pretty talented at the sport.Two of the most famous players in the footballing world right now are Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo.

CRICKET

Cricket is right up there in the list of the greatest sports along with football.Unlike Football which is played only for 90 minutes only, Cricket on the other hand is divided into 3 formats:

  • T20
    • It is a 20 over match.
    • Current world Champions : INDIA
  • ODI
    • It is a 50 over match.
    • Current World Chanpions : AUSTRALIA
  • Test
    • It is a 5 day match each day consisting of 90 overs.
    • Current World Champions : AUSTRALIA

England,Australia,India are the three countries that have been dominating this sport since a long time.However other nations like USA,Canada,UAE have been progressing significantly in the last couple of years.

The greatest cricketer of our era is said to be Virat Kohli.

FORMULA ONE

Formula One is a world racing championship where different drivers representing their team compete all year round to become the world champion.Each race takes place on different tracks all over the world.Some of the teams participating in formula one are :-

  • Mercedes
  • Red Bull
  • Ferrari
  • McLaren

Lewis Hamilton is currently the greatest formula one racecar driver with 8 world championships and over 100 wins to his name.However Max Verstappen has been rising up the ranks in the past few years winning races continuously.These two drivers have been dominating the sport over the last decade.
